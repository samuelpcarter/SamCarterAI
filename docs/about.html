About Me

Hi, I'm Sam Carter, a machine technician and robotics instructor from Carlsbad, California. At GKN Additive I maintain SLA, PolyJet and FDM printers used for custom manufacturing.

I've coordinated robotics programs with the Carlsbad Educational Foundation, mentoring dozens of student teams. Outside the shop I enjoy hiking — I've logged over 5,000 miles on long-distance trails — and supporting community STEM outreach.
